Hotel Sunset

250 km
Book Now

Description

The Hotel Sunset is a budget hotel with standardised amenities at the best value. The hotel offers excellent service and has an on-site restaurant. Located just 2 km from Agra Fort, this property is in close proximity to attractions including the Taj Mahal which can be reached in around 7 km.

Special Features

A cool room with AC, TV, free wifi and a geyser to take a nice bath. You can relax on the center table while surfing the internet.

The Hotel is in an excellent location in the center of town. The hotel has a variety of amenities to choose from, such as Housekeeping, Taxi Service, Laundry Service, Room Service and Fire-Extinguisher. We offer ticket tour assistance for our guests with available time slots.

Location & Transportation

The property is very close to several points of interest including Taj Mahal Garden(1 km), Taj Nature Walk(2 km), and Shri Mankameshwar Mandir(4 km). Agra Fort Railway Station is 4 km from the hotel. This hotel is only 8 km drive from Agra Airport.
